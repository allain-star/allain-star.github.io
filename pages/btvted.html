PROGRAM DESCRIPTION

The BTVTEd program is an Undergraduate teacher education program that equips learners with adequate and relevant competencies in teaching specific area in Industrial Arts and Home Economics or ICT or Agri-Fishery Arts - the four areas of technical and vocational track in the K to 12 Curriculum.

  • Computer Programming
  • Automotive Technology
  • Electronics Technology
  • Electrical Technology
  • Food and Service Management
  • Animation
